想到“人际交流”（可能是偏见，勿喷）：

- 剑桥（及英国文化）：没有“城墙”的礼貌

  AI说这里有一种 **礼貌文化 (culture of politeness)**。

  > - 陌生人之间会很自然地进行 **"small talk"** (闲聊)，比如在排队时评论一下天气。
  > - 无论在商店、公交车还是路上，你总会听到 "Sorry", "Thank you", "Cheers", "Excuse me"。
  > - 路人之间对视了，通常会给一个微笑或点头示意。
  > - 邻里之间见面了会打招呼，问一句 "You alright?" (你好吗？这是问候，不是真的问你有没有事)。

  > 英国人的这种表面热情，并不等于他想和你成为知心朋友。这是一种**公共礼仪 (public etiquette)**。

- 杭州（及国内大城市文化）：有“隔阂”的效率

  > 人们为了保护自己的**个人空间 (personal space)** 和提高效率，往往在公共场合会显得比较“冷漠”。你不会轻易和排队的陌生人聊天，邻里之间可能住了很久都互不认识。这种外在的“隔阂”其实是一种城市生活的自我保护机制。

## Crafting a Career in Tech: Panel Discussion

> https://www.meetup.com/cambridge-software-crafters/events/309865493

Timeline:

- 18:30 - 19:00 Network
- 19:00 - 20:30 Panel with Q&A
- 20:30 - 21:00 Network



IDEAS:

- Proactively seek opportunities rather than waiting

- AI developers should be acquainted with relevant legal knowledge, as this is crucial for making informed decisions regarding suitability and responsible development.

- For global expansion, the budget for employee relocation is often limited.

- Some recruiters value the potential, your passion and continuous learning.

- Selling: a kind of communication. Focus on selling your value rather than just selling your code.

- The ability to connect with people

- How to differentiate yourself:

  - Aim to generate passive opportunities by building a strong personal brand.
  - In an age where AI can automate tasks, your human connections become your key differentiator. Therefore, actively build your network on platforms like LinkedIn.

- Selling yourself, sharing sharing sharing

- Change your role Or grow your role. It's a question.

- A big company offers ample opportunities for you to pursue your interests. Moreover, there are often many problems that remain unresolved.

- Seek out individuals who are experiencing problems, listen to their challenges, and then demonstrate how you can provide a solution.

- Focus on acquiring a wide array of skills: people skills, social skills, and professional skills, among others.

- An interview is a process of mutual understanding.

- CV

  - prioritize clarity and a clean, well-organized layout. 
  - pay attention to subtle details like your email address; using a custom domain can make a more professional and memorable impression than a generic provider like Gmail.

- Side projects

  - The core value lies in the difficulty and significance of the problem you tackled, not just the specific technology you employed. 
  - Crucially, the project should be something you are genuinely interested in, as your passion will shine through.
